1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not track or log your search history
  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or IP address
  • We do not use tracking cookies or profiling technologies
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

2. Limited Data We Collect

To improve service reliability, Taisen Search may temporarily collect non-identifiable technical data, such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Language and display settings
  • Anonymous error logs (for debugging issues)
  • Search query performance metrics (without personal identifiers)

This information is used strictly for performance optimization and is never shared with third parties.

3. Use of Cookies

Taisen Search does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Minimal session cookies may be used only to maintain site functionality and are automatically deleted when you close your browser.

4. Third-Party Services

Our search results may include links to external websites. Taisen Search is not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal data.

5. Data Security

We take strong technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of information. Taisen Search uses HTTPS encryption to protect your connection and follows industry best practices for data security.

6. Children's Privacy

Taisen Search is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Taisen Search after changes indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
