Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2, 2025

At Taisen, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to keep your data secure when using our search services.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not track or log your search history.
  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or IP address.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or profiling technologies.

2. Limited Data We Collect

To improve service reliability, Taisen may temporarily collect non-identifiable technical data, such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Language and display settings
  • Anonymous error logs (for debugging issues)

This information is used strictly for performance optimization and is never shared with third parties.

3. Use of Cookies

Taisen does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Minimal session cookies may be used only to maintain site functionality and are automatically deleted when you close your browser.

4. Third-Party Services

Our search results may include links to external websites. Taisen is not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal data.

5. Data Security

We take strong technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of information. Taisen uses HTTPS encryption to protect your connection.

6. Children’s Privacy

Taisen is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Taisen after changes indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
